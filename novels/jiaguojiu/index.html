Synopsis

Li Yunzhu was a recognized beauty among the nobility, spoiled and willful, unable to tolerate the slightest grievance.

The young imperial uncle, Cao Shao, dreamed of marrying her, but unfortunately, his elders changed their minds.

“Yunzhu, it’s my incompetence that has wronged you.”
“Oh.”

Li Yunzhu didn’t have much affection for Cao Shao, but she couldn’t swallow this insult.

At this time, the eldest imperial uncle, Cao Xun, returned to the capital. He had said in his youth, “I won’t settle down until I’ve secured the borders.”

Now, the eldest imperial uncle, at thirty, had achieved great success, imposing and majestic.

Li Yunzhu: “Fine, I’ll marry him!”
